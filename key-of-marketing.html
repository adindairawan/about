The Key to Marketing Lies in The Human Heart

BY ADINDA IRAWAN

Hunderend textbooks, thousands of journals, Millions of articles had discussed the most important aspect of marketing and how to sell products. Of all of those millions of information the foremost important essence of marketing is simply empathy.


The basic of human nature

Humans are a social creature, They want to have a personal bond with the brands they associate themselves with like theyâ€™re being cared about by someone close to them like relatives or friends. A good business or product function is to help their customer in a certain activity, to asset their life or make them feel good. We need to be as human as possible in the marketing aspect so that we get that bond with the customer to the brand themself. Humanizing your advertising builds confidence. It taps into the emotions of individuals, and research shows that this is one of the most important elements of the buyer's journey's decision-making process.

To put ourself in the shoe of the customer, know what their beliefs, their inside joke, their niche preferences. It's much easier if you yourself are one of the targeted customers but that doesn't mean that you have to be, you could always research, ask the target, and ask yourself if I were them what would I do?


What are the basic things?

Few of the general and important tips in most marketing is that first and foremost is that brands need to talk like an actual person in the aspect of digital and traditional marketing. And be transparent, about the product development or services. Be a good listener, be proactive, and make conversation with the customer. Lastly, tell a story with your brand, great story build authenticity to the brand and build a strong relationship.

It begins with the empathy to break out of the stiff corporate-centered mentality to a more fluid helping customer-centered brand personality. The best marketing and selling feels like helping and well it's because it is.
